Our Services

DHIN drives digital health transformation by advocating for policies that promote interoperability, data governance, and global health standards. We implement international standards like HL7 FHIR and SNOMED CT, and work to integrate health systems, eliminating data silos. Through initiatives like the Testing Sandbox and Connectathon, we enable real-world testing of interoperability solutions. DHIN fosters collaboration among policymakers, developers, healthcare providers, and IT workers to create a more connected digital health ecosystem across Africa.

Policy Advocacy

At DHIN, we recognize that policy plays a critical role in driving digital health transformation. Our advocacy efforts focus on promoting interoperability standards, improving regional data governance, and the adoption of global digital health standards. We actively engage with regulatory bodies, government agencies, and global health organizations to shape policies that facilitate seamless data exchange, protect patient privacy, and enhance healthcare service delivery.

Standardization

Standardization is at the core of DHIN's mission. We work to accelerate the adoption and implementation of international health data standards such as HL7 FHIR, SNOMED CT, LOINC, and ICD-10. We work closely with health IT developers, government institutions, and healthcare providers to ensure that digital health systems adhere to these standards, enabling seamless data exchange and improving healthcare outcomes.

Integration

A core focus of DHIN is ensuring that digital health solutions can communicate with each other, eliminating data silos that hinder effective healthcare delivery. We drive efforts to integrate disparate health information systems, ensuring they work together to provide a unified and efficient healthcare ecosystem. We have various initiatives, including Testing Sandbox approach and annual Connectathon, where we provide real-world environments where stakeholders can test, validate, and refine their interoperability solutions.

Collaboration

Collaboration is at the heart of DHIN's approach. We bring together policymakers, health IT developers, healthcare providers, and other stakeholders to create a community-driven ecosystem that drives interoperability. Through our Community of Practice (CoP) sessions, strategic partnerships, and mentorship programs, we facilitate an ecosystem where knowledge sharing and capacity building thrive.
