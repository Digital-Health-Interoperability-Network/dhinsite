DHIN Community

Collaborating to Advance Digital Health Interoperability Together.

What we are About?

The Digital Health Interoperability DHIN Community (DHINC) is a collaborative forum for professionals, organizations, and stakeholders passionate about improving healthcare data exchange. Our mission is to share knowledge, drive innovation, and solve real-world challenges in healthcare interoperability.

What you stand to Gain

Information Sessions: Participate in thought-provoking discussions on topics such as EHR interoperability, telemedicine, patient data security, and more.
Interactive Events: Attend webinars, workshops, and meetups led by industry experts, addressing cutting-edge digital health challenges.
Innovation Spotlights: Explore and share innovations that are revolutionizing healthcare delivery and patient outcomes.
Community Spotlights: Showcase your expertise, projects, and successes, and learn from others' experiences.
Ask the experts: Have burning questions? Our community is a place to seek advice and insights from those who've been there.
Exclusive Updates: Be the first to know about DHIN's initiatives, research findings, and industry partnerships.

Who can join us

Membership is free and open to:

  • Healthcare Professionals
  • Technology Innovators
  • Researchers and Academics
  • Policy Makers
  • Students and all Digital Health Enthusiasts and Interns
