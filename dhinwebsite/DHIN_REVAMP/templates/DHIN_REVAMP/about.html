About DHIN

Welcome to the Digital Health Interoperability Network (DHIN), where our commitment to universal health coverage drives us to foster an ecosystem of seamless integration.

Our Mission

At the Digital Health Interoperability Network (DHIN), our mission is to accelerate universal health coverage by fostering an enabling environment for seamless integration of diverse digital health interventions.

We are dedicated to creating an ecosystem where standardized digital solutions can seamlessly interoperate, enabling health stakeholders to access and utilize critical information efficiently.

Guiding Principles

Disruptive

We're not afraid to shake things up. Our drive to disrupt traditional approaches to health interoperability by bringing stakeholders together and doing.

Evidence-Based Problem Solving

We roll up our sleeves and dig into challenges. We drive evidence of standards and interoperability. Armed with evidence, we tackle issues head-on, employing hands-on practical approaches. We believe in learning from failures and seeing setbacks as opportunities.

Collaborative & Peer-Driven

We are not just a team; we are a community. We believe in co-creation and the power of collaboration. Our peer-driven approach ensures that diverse voices contribute to approaches that truly work for everyone.

Our Core Values

  • Integrity: We uphold honesty and transparency in all actions.
  • Equity: We ensure fairness and inclusivity for all.
  • Excellence: We deliver high-quality solutions that surpass expectations.
  • Learning: We foster continuous growth and the exploration of new ideas.
  • Trust: We build and maintain confidence through integrity.
  • Transparency: We communicate openly and take responsibility for our actions.
  • Reliability: We consistently deliver on our commitments

Governance Structure

Trustees

The DHIN trustee board is made up of three co-conveners with over 50 years of combined experience in clinical practice, engineering, computer science, and hands-on leading and driving digital health program design, collaboration, and implementation in a few dozen countries, mainly in Africa.

Advisory Board

DHIN Advisory Board is made up of experts in public health, clinical practice, ICT, and digital health. Members are handpicked through nominations or applications, fostering a rich tapestry of perspectives.

DHIN Community of Practice (CoP)

We welcome healthcare professionals, clinicians, government, academia, developers, and other stakeholders who share a passionate commitment for connected healthcare. If you possess experience in digital or health interoperability projects, we eagerly invite you to join.
