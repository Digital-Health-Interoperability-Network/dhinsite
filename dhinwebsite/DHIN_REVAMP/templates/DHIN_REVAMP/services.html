Our Services

DHIN drives digital health transformation by advocating for policies that promote interoperability, data governance, and global health standards. We implement international standards like HL7 FHIR and SNOMED CT, and work to integrate health systems, eliminating data silos. Through initiatives like the Testing Sandbox and Connectathon, we enable real-world testing of interoperability solutions. DHIN fosters collaboration among policymakers, developers, healthcare providers, and global organizations, building an ecosystem that accelerates digital health adoption and enhances healthcare delivery across Africa.


Policy Advocacy

At DHIN, we recognize that policy plays a critical role in driving digital health transformation. Our advocacy efforts focus on influencing policy decisions that promote interoperability, health data governance, and the adoption of global digital health standards. We actively engage with regulatory bodies, government agencies, and global health organizations to shape policies that facilitate seamless data exchange, protect patient privacy, and enhance healthcare service delivery. Through stakeholder engagements, policy dialogues, and collaborative working groups, we champion regulatory reforms that support the sustainable growth of digital health in low-resource settings.

Standardization

Interoperability cannot exist without standardization. DHIN is firmly committed to adoption and implementation of international health data standards such as HL7 FHIR, SNOMED CT, LOINC, and ICD-11. We work closely with health IT developers, government institutions, and healthcare providers to ensure that digital health systems adhere to these standards, enabling seamless data exchange and improving healthcare outcomes. Our technical working groups and training sessions focus on equipping stakeholders with the knowledge and tools needed to implement standard-compliant solutions, reducing data silos and fragmentation in health systems.
